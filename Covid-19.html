COVID-19 is the disease caused by the new coronavirus known as SARS-CoV-2. The WHO first learned of the existence of this new virus on December 31, 2019, when it was informed of a group of cases of "viral pneumonia" that had been declared in Wuhan (People's Republic of China).

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are:

  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Tiredness

Other less frequent symptoms that may affect some patients:

  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Nasal congestion
  • Conjunctivitis (eye redness)
  • Throat pain
  • Headache
  • Muscle or joint pain
  • Different types of skin rashes
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Chills or vertigo

Symptoms of severe COVID-19 include:

  • Dyspnea (shortness of breath)
  • Loss of appetite
  • Confusion
  • Persistent chest pain or tightness
  • High temperature (above 38 ° C)

Other less frequent symptoms:

  • Loss of consciousness (sometimes associated with seizures)
  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Sleep disorders

More serious and rare neurological complications, such as strokes, brain swelling, delusional state, and neural injuries. People of any age who have a fever or cough and who are also short of breath, have chest pain or tightness, or have difficulty speaking or moving should seek medical attention immediately. If possible, call your health care provider, helpline or health center in advance so that they can direct you to the appropriate dispensary.
